*These rules define the boundaries of our service and are used for strict qualification.*

### 1.1 Minimum Project Thresholds
- **Policy_Budget_Minimum**: The absolute minimum investment for any renovation project is **R850,000**. Projects falling below this value are referred to our "DIY & Budgeting Guide" PDF.
- **Rule_Service_Area**: We exclusively service the following areas: Constantia, Bishopscourt, Fresnaye, Bantry Bay, Camps Bay, and Llandudno.
- **Condition_Property_Ownership**: We only engage with the legal owner of the property. We do not perform consultations for tenants or prospective buyers without a signed Offer to Purchase.

- **Rule_Lead_Time**: A minimum of 48 hours notice is required for all on-site consultations.
- **Rule_Booking_Restriction**: No same-day bookings are permitted. The earliest available slot will always be the next business day.
